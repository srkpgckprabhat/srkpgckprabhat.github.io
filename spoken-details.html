Spoken English, Personality Development & Interview Guidance Workshop

A Comprehensive 10-Day Skill Enhancement Program

The Spoken English, Personality Development & Interview Guidance Workshop is a carefully designed 10-day training program aimed at transforming students into confident communicators and well-rounded individuals. This workshop focuses on enhancing spoken English skills, building personality traits, and preparing students to excel in interviews and real-life situations.

🎯 Objectives of the Workshop

  • Improve spoken English and communication skills
  • Develop confidence in public speaking
  • Enhance personality traits and self-presentation
  • Prepare students for interviews
  • Eliminate hesitation and fear
  • Build leadership qualities and positive thinking

📅 Duration and Structure

  • Duration: 10 Days (Intensive Program)
  • Mode: Interactive and activity-based learning
  • Focus: Practical communication
  • Approach: Daily practice and feedback

Each day is structured to gradually improve fluency, confidence, and personality.

📘 Course Curriculum Overview

Day 1: Introduction & Confidence Building

  • Importance of communication
  • Overcoming fear
  • Basic speaking practice

Day 2: Fundamentals of Spoken English

  • Grammar in speaking
  • Sentence formation
  • Error correction

Day 3: Vocabulary & Fluency

  • Daily vocabulary
  • Fluency practice
  • Speaking exercises

Day 4: Pronunciation Training

  • Correct pronunciation
  • Stress and tone
  • Listening drills

Day 5: Conversation Skills

  • Real-life conversations
  • Group discussions
  • Confidence building

Day 6: Personality Development

  • Body language
  • Grooming
  • First impressions

Day 7: Public Speaking

  • Stage confidence
  • Speech structure
  • Practice sessions

Day 8: Interview Preparation

  • Types of interviews
  • Common questions
  • Answer techniques

Day 9: Mock Interviews

  • Simulation practice
  • Feedback
  • Evaluation

Day 10: Final Presentation

  • Final speech
  • Evaluation
  • Certificate distribution

🏆 Special Recognition

A special “Dr. Aradhana Award for Excellence in Public Speaking” is awarded to the most outstanding performer, recognizing confidence, clarity, and impactful communication.

This workshop is not just about learning English—it is about transforming personality and unlocking confidence. Students emerge as confident speakers ready to succeed in every sphere of life.
