Dr. J. P. Shukla

Principal | Academic Leader | Visionary Administrator

Dr. J. P. Shukla is a distinguished academician and the Principal of Shri Ratanlal Kanwarlal Patni Government P.G. College, Kishangarh. Known for his visionary leadership, administrative excellence, and commitment to academic growth, he has played a pivotal role in transforming the institution into a dynamic center of learning and innovation.

His leadership is defined by:

  • Efficient academic and administrative management
  • Student-centric decision-making

He fosters an environment where both faculty and students can grow, explore, and excel.
