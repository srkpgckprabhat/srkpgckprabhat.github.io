Artificial Intelligence Workshop

A Comprehensive Skill-Based Training Program for Students

The Artificial Intelligence Workshop is a structured, hands-on training initiative designed to introduce students to practical applications of AI and Large Language Models in academics, daily life, and professional development. Conducted in collaboration with Programming Express Private Limited, the workshop bridges the gap between theory and real-world skills.

🎯 Objectives of the Workshop

  • Understand fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence
  • Develop practical skills using AI tools like ChatGPT
  • Create real-world digital projects
  • Enhance creativity and problem-solving
  • Explore career opportunities in AI
  • Build professional portfolios and certifications

📅 Duration and Structure

  • Duration: 5 Days (Intensive)
  • Timing: 11:00 AM – 12:30 PM
  • Mode: Hands-on practical training
  • Venue: Knowledge Centre
  • Batch Size: Limited

Each day builds progressively for effective learning even for beginners.

📘 Course Curriculum Overview

Day 1: Foundations of AI

  • Introduction and evolution of AI
  • Neural Networks
  • Large Language Models
  • Impact on society

Day 2: AI in Academics

  • Using ChatGPT effectively
  • Notes, summaries, study plans
  • AI as research assistant

Day 3: Website Creation

  • Generate code using AI
  • HTML basics
  • Multi-page websites
  • GitHub deployment

Day 4: Advanced Projects

  • Interactive projects
  • Games, quizzes, applications
  • Navigation and structure

Day 5: Future & Careers

  • AI careers and certifications
  • SWAYAM, Coursera, Google, IBM
  • Continuous learning strategies

🚀 Advanced Learning (Batch 2)

  • Custom domain setup (.com)
  • Payment gateway integration
  • Monetization (AdSense, traffic)
  • Earning using digital skills

🧪 Learning Methodology

  • Hands-on sessions
  • Step-by-step guidance

  • Interactive discussions
