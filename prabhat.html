Prabhat Yashi

Assistant Professor of English | Educator | AI Trainer | Mentor

Known for his innovative teaching methods, deep philosophical insight, and strong command over both language and emerging technologies, he represents a unique blend of traditional scholarship and modern skill development.

🎓 Academic and Professional Excellence

He has cleared multiple prestigious government examinations:
