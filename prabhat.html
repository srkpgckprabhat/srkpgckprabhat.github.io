Prabhat Yashi – Program Co-ordinator

Prabhat Yashi Assistant Professor of English | Educator | AI Trainer | Mentor Prabhat Yashi is a dynamic and visionary educator currently serving as an Assistant Professor of English in a government institution in Rajasthan. Known for his innovative teaching methods, deep philosophical insight, and strong command over both language and emerging technologies, he represents a unique blend of traditional scholarship and modern skill development. --- 🎓 Academic and Professional Excellence Prabhat Yashi has demonstrated exceptional academic capability by clearing multiple prestigious government examinations, including: RAS (Rajasthan Administrative Services) RPSC 1st Grade Lecturer Examination RPSC Assistant Professor Examination In addition, he has qualified NET-JRF, reflecting his strong research aptitude and academic depth. He holds a Master’s degree in English Literature and is currently pursuing his PhD, focusing on a comparative study involving Charles Darwin, Sri Aurobindo’s Savitri, and Yuval Noah Harari’s Homo Deus. --- 📚 Teaching Philosophy and Approach His teaching philosophy is rooted in the idea that students are not containers to be filled, but lamps to be lit. He emphasizes curiosity, critical thinking, and real-world application of knowledge. Despite the challenges of visual impairment caused by optic nerve atrophy, he has developed a powerful memory-based teaching system and an engaging oral pedagogy. He actively involves students in discussions, listening-based learning, and interactive exercises, making education both accessible and impactful. He integrates contemporary examples from popular culture, technology, and real-life scenarios to simplify complex literary and theoretical concepts. His classrooms are known for generating curiosity, encouraging questions, and fostering intellectual independence. --- 🤖 Pioneer in AI-Based Education Prabhat Yashi has emerged as a pioneer in integrating Artificial Intelligence into classroom learning, especially in government institutions. He has successfully designed and conducted multiple Artificial Intelligence Workshops, where students learn: Practical use of AI tools like ChatGPT Website creation and deployment Game development using AI Research and academic applications of AI Monetization techniques using digital skills Under his guidance, students have created real-world projects such as websites and games, some of which have even received media recognition. His initiative has positioned his institution among the early adopters of structured AI training in Rajasthan. --- 🏫 Leadership and Educational Initiatives He actively leads and coordinates various academic and skill-development programs, including: Artificial Intelligence Workshops (multi-batch structured programs) Spoken English, Personality Development, and Interview Guidance Workshops Free coaching initiatives for government school students Encouragement of AI-based self-learning among students He is also associated with initiatives that aim to bridge the gap between rural and urban education by providing high-quality skill training at minimal or no cost. --- 🧠 Intellectual and Philosophical Depth Prabhat Yashi possesses deep knowledge of multiple philosophical and religious traditions, including: Advaita Vedanta Buddhism Christianity Islam and Judaism He finds intellectual and emotional strength in the Upanishads and integrates philosophical wisdom into his teaching, helping students develop not just academically, but also as thoughtful individuals. --- 💡 Vision and Impact His vision is to empower students—especially from government institutions—with skills that are relevant in the modern world. He believes in: Democratizing access to advanced technologies like AI Building self-reliance among students Encouraging innovation and entrepreneurship Creating a generation of confident, skilled, and ethically grounded individuals --- 🌟 Personal Strength and Inspiration Despite facing complete vision loss, Prabhat Yashi has transformed adversity into strength. His journey is a powerful example of resilience, determination, and purpose-driven life. He continues to inspire students not just through his knowledge, but through his life itself—proving that limitations can be transcended with willpower, discipline, and faith. --- 🏆 Conclusion Prabhat Yashi is not just an educator—he is a mentor, innovator, and change-maker. His work stands at the intersection of literature, philosophy, and technology, making him a rare and impactful figure in contemporary education. Through his efforts, countless students are not only learning but evolving—academically, professionally, and personally—towards a brighter and more empowered future.
